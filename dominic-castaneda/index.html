My Introduction

I'm Dominic Castaneda, a passionate Computer Science student at Macomb Community College, soon to be at Oakland University in 2024. My academic journey is fueled by an interest in software engineering, cybersecurity, and AI. I've been working on my skills in Python, C++, Linux, HTML, and CSS. Not only have I been practicing in the classroom but also in personal projects, such as developing a website for my landscaping business (Undergrad Landscaping), creating programs for it, and this personal portfolio. As I continue to expand my knowledge and skills, I am actively seeking internship opportunities in software engineering, cybersecurity, or AI. I'm excited to connect with professionals and peers in the tech industry, so feel free to reach out for any collaboration, advice, or opportunities!
